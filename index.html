About

Stoffel Recording Company is a Music Recording, Mixing, Mastering, and Production Company based out of Nashville, TN. It is owned and operated by Audio Engineer Jordan Stoffel. Jordan grew up in Huntington, Indiana where he became enamored with music at a very young age and began as a musician. During his time in the United States Navy, his focus changed toward the recording and production aspect of music. After his contract ended, he spent 4 years studying Audio Recording and Production at the University of Saint Francis while working in technical support at Sweetwater Sound, Inc. After Graduation, Jordan relocated to Nashville, TN to work at Blackbird Studio and create Stoffel Recording Company.

Jordan has over 7 years of Recording and Production experience for various genres and is able to sonically sculpt your music ideas into a product that will keep listeners hooked.

Stoffel Recording Co. Provides Recording, Mixing, Mastering, and Production services for all genres.
