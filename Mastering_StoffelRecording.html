What is Mastering?

Mastering is the most enigmatic part of creating a song, yet absolutely the most important. Even if a song is recorded and mixed well, it will not be well-received by listeners if it is improperly Mastered.

Stoffel Recording Co. Masters are done both in and out of the Box with various outboard analog compressors and EQs, as well as hundreds of software plugins and Mastering Suite options. Metadata including album artwork can be imbedded into files upon request.

Your mix will sound bigger and wider with improvements in the overall tonal quality (if necessary), and of course it will be made as loud as possible while maintaining a rich, healthy dynamic range that will put comparable songs to shame.
